The Goal

2020-05-10

What is the point of this?

Mission • To Experiment, Document, and Augment

16 years old and still developing, my brain is in a rapid state of growth as I approach adulthood. I wanted to start this website because I believe that in this modern era, people unknowingly overstimulate themselves as they indulge in technological advancements primarily in entertainment. Our primitive lymbic system loves the binging and distracing as it’s primary purpose is to simply keep us alive. The rational mind tells us that one more episode should only mean one last episode but sometimes we can’t help but say that ten sounds better. We are a complex machine with our brain serving as the most powerful processer with over eighty-six billion neurons. We have the ability to adapt like no other creature and yet so many people avoid the idea of self development. I decided that I would use this website as a journal through my various self experiments as I attempt to improve myself.
